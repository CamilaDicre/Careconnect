Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

At Careconnect, we collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect include:

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Date of birth and emergency contact information
  • Health information and medical history (with your consent)
  • Caregiver qualifications and experience

Usage Information

  • How you use our platform and services
  • Device information and IP address
  • Cookies and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our caregiving services
  • To match caregivers with patients based on needs and qualifications
  • To send important notifications about appointments and health updates
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With healthcare providers involved in your care
  • To comply with legal requirements or court orders
  • To protect the safety and security of our users
  • With service providers who help us operate our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication measures
  • Secure data centers with physical and digital protection

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent at any time

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences or our cookie policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our platform and updating the "Last updated" date.
