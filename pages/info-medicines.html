Malestares

Stomach Ache

  • Antacid (Tums, Rolaids) - Neutralizes stomach acid
  • Simethicone (Gas-X) - Relieves gas and bloating
  • Bismuth Subsalicylate (Pepto-Bismol) - Treats upset stomach
  • Omeprazole (Prilosec) - Reduces acid production

Hipertension

  • ACE Inhibitors (Lisinopril) - Relaxes blood vessels
  • Beta Blockers (Metoprolol) - Reduces heart rate
  • Calcium Channel Blockers (Amlodipine) - Relaxes arteries
  • Diuretics (Hydrochlorothiazide) - Removes excess fluid

Headache

  • Acetaminophen (Tylenol) - Pain reliever, gentle on stomach
  • Ibuprofen (Advil, Motrin) - Anti-inflammatory pain reliever
  • Aspirin - Pain reliever with anti-inflammatory
  • Sumatriptan (Imitrex) - For migraine headaches

Colds

  • Decongestants (Pseudoephedrine) - Reduces nasal congestion
  • Antihistamines (Diphenhydramine) - Helps runny nose and sneezing
  • Cough Suppressants (Dextromethorphan) - Reduces coughing
  • Expectorants (Guaifenesin) - Thins mucus

Gastritis

  • Proton Pump Inhibitors (Omeprazole) - Reduces stomach acid
  • H2 Blockers (Ranitidine, Famotidine) - Decreases acid production
  • Antacids (Mylanta, Maalox) - Neutralizes stomach acid
  • Sucralfate (Carafate) - Protects stomach lining

Joint Pain

  • NSAIDs (Ibuprofen, Naproxen) - Anti-inflammatory for joints
  • Topical pain relievers (Voltaren Gel) - Applied directly to skin
  • Acetaminophen (Tylenol) - Pain reliever, easier on stomach
  • Glucosamine/Chondroitin - Supplements for joint health
