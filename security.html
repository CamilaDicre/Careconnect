Our Security Commitment

At Careconnect, we understand that health information is among the most sensitive personal data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and security with industry-leading measures that exceed healthcare industry standards. Your trust is our highest priority.

Data Protection Standards

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

Encryption & Data Protection

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit
  • TLS 1.3 secure connections for all communications
  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive health data
  • Database encryption with industry-standard algorithms

Access Control & Authentication

  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all accounts
  • Role-based access controls with least privilege principles
  • Biometric authentication support for mobile devices
  • Session management with automatic timeouts

Infrastructure Security

  • Cloud security with AWS/Azure enterprise-grade protection
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • 24/7 monitoring and threat detection
  • Automated backups with geographic redundancy

Compliance & Certifications

We maintain compliance with the highest security and privacy standards:

  • HIPAA Compliance: Full compliance with Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act
  • GDPR Compliance: European General Data Protection Regulation
  • ISO 27001: Information Security Management System certification
  • SOC 2 Type II: Service Organization Control 2 certification
  • HITECH Act: Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health compliance

  • Regular audits: Ongoing monitoring of third-party security
  • Limited access: Minimal data sharing with trusted partners only

Security Best Practices for Users

Help us keep your account secure by following these best practices:

  • Strong passwords: Use unique, complex passwords for your account
  • Enable MFA: Always use multi-factor authentication
  • Regular updates: Keep your devices and apps updated
  • Secure networks: Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities
  • Logout properly: Always log out when using shared devices
  • Report suspicious activity: Contact us immediately if you notice anything unusual
